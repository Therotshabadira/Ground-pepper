About Ground Pepper Catering

From the Ground to Your Table, Crafted with Care

Our Story

Ground Pepper Catering Services was founded in 2010 with the vision of bringing premium catering experiences to events big and small. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most sought-after catering services in the region.

Our founder, Leatile Tshabadira, began her culinary journey at a young age, learning traditional recipes from her grandmother. This passion for authentic, flavorful food became the foundation of our catering service.

Our Mission

To create unforgettable culinary experiences by combining exceptional food with outstanding service, tailored to each client's unique vision.

Our Values

  • Quality ingredients sourced locally
  • Attention to detail in every dish
  • Professional and friendly service
  • Customized solutions for each event
